PVP (Player Verses Player)

Player verses Player gameplay, usually referred to as PVP, is the online battling environment where you can battle against other Trainers around the world to be the very best! Completion of these battles will earn you various rewards, including stardust, TMs (used to teach your Pokemon different attacks), and encounters with various pokemon. These encounters will have boosted IVs, increasing the chances of perfect stat Pokemon.

DISCLAIMER! PVP is incredibly rewarding, but tends to have a steep learning curve and requires lots of resources including stardust, candy, and patience. Competitive battling can be discouraging, but it is well worth the effort in the long run.
